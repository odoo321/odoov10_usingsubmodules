ODOO Property Commission

Odoo Property Commission Management System will make your account keeping of commission more cozy and hazzle free.

Commission Invoice

All the commission related accounts and transactions are being recorded and its invoice can be generated for corresponding users.

Commission Report

Depending on the price of property and for all sort of service and maintenance ,commission can be calculated and take print of record with in the time interval of service.

Commission Type

Any type of commission can be accepted through Property Commission whether it is percentage ,fixed or whatever else.
