ODOO Property Maintenance

Odoo Property Maintenance can foster your maintenance status and will make more ease and chaos free than ever before.

Round the clock maintenance of property

odoo property maintenance can keep the maintenance records ,maintenance stages extreamely obivious, and up to date status can be reported and executed parallelly

Maintenance Status View

The Maintenance Status will show the whole episode of maintenance record and its current status,it also explains what sort of maintenance is been done from the beginning to end of cycle.

Maintenance Calendar View

The Maintenance Calendar will show the up to date status of each and every maintenance process with date of assignment and date of completion

Maintenance Process

Here we can see how the maintenance cycle is been carried out and how does it change according to the user request.

Maintenance Team

Personnel's list who are expertised in several kinds of jobs can be visible in the Maintenance Team according to his/her experience in corresponding domain, we can assign job to them and their work status will be updated
